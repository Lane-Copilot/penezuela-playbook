üöÄ Step-by-Step: Getting Started

1
Buy a printer ($200-600)
Start with the Bambu Lab A1 Mini ($299) or Ender-3 V3 SE ($199). One printer is enough to start. Hit up Micro Center in Kendall.
2
Pick ONE niche
Don't try to sell everything. Pick one: home decor, gaming accessories, pet stuff, or cosplay. Master it.
3
Find/design 5-10 products
Use Thingiverse, Printables, or MyMiniFactory for free/commercial STL files. Or learn Fusion 360 (free for personal use) to design your own ‚Äî custom = higher margins.
4
Open Etsy shop + TikTok Shop
Etsy: $0.20 per listing. TikTok Shop: free to join. Run BOTH ‚Äî Etsy for search traffic, TikTok for viral discovery.
5
Create content ‚Äî film the printing process
Time-lapses of 3D prints go VIRAL on TikTok. Film the process, show the finished product. This is your free marketing machine.
6
Set up Pirate Ship for shipping
Connect to your Etsy shop for auto-imported orders. Print labels at home. Drop packages at USPS.
7
Scale: add printers + automate
Once you're selling consistently, add a second printer. Set up OctoPrint on a Raspberry Pi for remote monitoring. Eventually build a mini print farm (3-5 printers).
